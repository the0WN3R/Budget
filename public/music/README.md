l# Background Music Files

To add custom background music for each style:

## Required Files

Place the following MP3 files in this directory (`/public/music/`):

1. **soft-muted.mp3** - Peaceful classic grand piano music
2. **funky-pop.mp3** - Upbeat and energetic pop/funk music
3. **classical.mp3** - Orchestra and symphony music

## Where to Get Music

### Soft & Muted (Grand Piano)
- Look for "peaceful piano", "ambient piano", or "lobby piano" music
- Examples: Pixabay Music, FreePD, Incompetech (Kevin MacLeod)

### Classical (Orchestra/Symphony)
- Look for "orchestral", "symphony", or "classical orchestra" music
- Examples: Pixabay Music, FreePD, MusOpen

### Funky/Pop
- Look for "upbeat", "funky", or "pop background" music
- Examples: Pixabay Music, FreePD, Bensound

## File Format
- Format: MP3
- Recommended: 128-192 kbps, stereo
- Duration: Can be any length (will loop automatically)

## License
Make sure all music files are royalty-free and properly licensed for use in your application.
